About

Hi, I'm Rietesh.

My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, geospatial intelligence, and distributed systems, with a focus on designing solutions that are not just accurate, but reliable and deployable at scale.

My expertise lies in developing mission-critical embedded systems and extracting structured insights from large-scale spatial data. I design architectures that prioritize understanding how software, sensor data, and physical infrastructure interact under real-world, adversarial constraints.

I am deeply focused on defense tech and deep-tech applications—where system reliability, model interpretability, and operational robustness matter far more than theoretical performance. My goal is to engineer resilient systems that operate seamlessly in challenging, denied, or contested environments, integrating across hardware and software boundaries to deliver strategic, real-world impact.
